# Ars Astrologique

A Work-in-Progress add-on for the Ars Nouveau mod themed around astronomy and space magic!

## Enchanter
A brand-new trader mob focused on spellcasting accessories and basic magical essences. Trades may include items from famous Ars Nouveau add-ons like Ars Additions, Ars Elemental, Ars Technica, and Ars Zero! (Add-ons must be downloaded separately). Tuning Forks from Ars Ingressum also on sale.

## New Glyphs
* **Alacrity (Tier 3)** Grants the target overall increased speeds (mining, attacking, and movement). Careful, though! This glyph has a backlash. If it ever runs out, you'll be stuck in place and unable to mine anything.
* **Bless (Tier 3)** Increases all incoming healing to the target by 20%, to a maximum of 100%.
* **Empower (Tier 2)** Increases melee attack damage and knockback, as well as jumping strength and safe falling distances.
* **False Life (Tier 2)** Grants absorption to the target.
* **Invigorate (Tier 3)** Passively recovers hunger and saturation for the target. This allows for prompt regeneration on the spot.
* **Stalwart (Tier 3)** Grants heavy damage and knockback reduction at the cost of heavy movement speed reduction. Stalwart V makes you a temporary immovable object incapable of being killed by normal means.
